About Us

Every empty glass is filled with a great story!

Corfe Castle Club

A friendly club in the village center, formerly a school and built in Purbeck stone. The main bar has upholstered bench seating, TV for major sporting events, darts and Purbeck longboard shove ha’penny.

  • An upstairs room has a pool table and can be hired for meetings. Filled rolls are available all day.
  • The spectacular garden boasts a boules court and views over the Purbeck hills.
  • Convenient for the Castle or Steam Railway

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Are you open to non members?

    Yes we are. If your just down for a trip or staying with friends, or passing through we'd be happy to see you.
    If you have moved to the village and looking to pop in more often then why not take out membership?'

  • How much is membership?

    Initially membership is £11.00 for the year with £1.00 going on administration. Renewal of your membership is just £10.00 and don't forget it saves you money in the long run.

  • What time are you open?

    Through the winter we are open from 12.00 til 16.00 and again in the evening from 19.00 until 23.00. Summer we open from 11.00 til 23.00. We are open Bank holidays, most of the festive season including part of Christmas Day. As well as doing a late licence for private functions.

  • How much is it to book the function room?

    Prices range from £30.00 for a family function such as a birthday party. To £100.00 for a larger gathering or wedding reception. We can supply both drinks and food as well as knowing many local bands and entertainers. Give us a ring and see what we can do.

  • Do You have sky Sports?

    We have a range of sports and movie channels and host all the big games on our large screen. We also have a projector for the summer months to show live coverage of the big games such as the world cup football, Rugby, Cricket and Wimbeldon to name but a few.

  • What is transport like to and from Corfe?

    We are on the main bus route to Poole and Swanage and the local train station is only a mile up the road. There are also a lot of local taxi companies that cover all over Dorset for a reasonable price. There ar also walking routes througout the village over to Church Knowle, Wareham and across the common to Swanage and Arne.
